Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play?
Tap the screen to flap. Avoid the pipes and try to beat your high score.

Does the game collect data?
Flappy YOU stores high scores locally and does not collect personal data.

Which devices are supported?
iPhone and iPad devices running iOS 15 or later.

Privacy Policy

Flappy YOU does not collect, store, or transmit personal information.
